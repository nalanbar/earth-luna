Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 March, 2026

Overview

The world is already a bad enough place. We are playing a game about plucky humans choosing to put their own money and bodies on the line to interfere with the agendas of megacorporations and alien invaders. We don’t want to make it worse by having access to your data, and somehow making a mistake with it. This document will describe in length how we do not collect your data.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data from you, unless you choose to donate money to us. In that instance, we will see your email address or screen name. You do not need to tell us anything or give us anything to use this app. This includes but is not limited to your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying information.

How We Use Your Data

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to use.

Data Storage

The app stores state data locally on your device. We do not transmit or store any of this data on our servers. If you choose to donate, we use a third-party payment processor to handle the transaction, and they may have their own data storage practices. Please refer to their privacy policy for more information.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising services. The only third-party service we use is a payment processor for donations, and we do not have any data to share with them - they would be sharing your data with us, not the other way around. We recommend reviewing their privacy policy for details on how they handle your information.

Your Rights

If you have unlimited rights to do anything you want with your data, but since we don’t have any of it, there isn’t anything to do with it. If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section below.

Children’s Privacy

I am a father, and I go very far out of my own way to make sure that my children are safe online. I do not want to be the reason that any child is put in danger. Therefore, I do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
