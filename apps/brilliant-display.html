Brilliant Display

Brilliant Display is a companion app for Ashes Reborn and Ashes Ascendancy. It brings collection tracking, deck building, and fast card browsing into one place, with a presentation that tries to honor just how beautiful this game already is.

Collection tracking Deck building Card browser

Know what you own

Keep track of the releases in your collection and build a shopping list for the ones you still need. Your favorite magic type also shapes the look of the app, so the interface can feel a little more personal.

Build here, browse anywhere

Brilliant Display lets you browse public decks, keep local drafts on your device, and connect to Ashes.live when you want to move between private planning and community sharing.

Made for reading cards well

Search the card pool, read the details quickly, and move from cards to decks without losing context. The goal is simple: less friction when you want to look something up, compare options, or start building.
