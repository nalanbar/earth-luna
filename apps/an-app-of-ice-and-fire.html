Working Title

An App of Ice and Fire

Night gathers, and now your watch begins.

An App of Ice and Fire is a companion app for collection tracking, deck building, and card reading for A Game of Thrones: Second Edition. It is built for players and collectors who still care deeply about the game and want a modern tool for keeping that community knowledge close at hand.

Collection tracking Deck building Card browser

Track the collection you actually have

Keep up with cycles and packs, deluxe expansions, and community releases in one place. The app is designed to make it easy to see what is already on your shelf and what you still want to hunt down.

There is even a shopping list tool that exports a list of pieces missing from your collection that you can easily share with your loved ones.

Read cards and browse decks with context

Search the card pool, browse decks, and move from collection tracking into deck building without losing your place. ThronesDB data helps keep the app useful whether you are studying the card pool or putting together your next list.

An app for this night, and all the nights to come

A Game of Thrones: Second Edition may no longer be supported by its original publisher, but its community is still active, knowledgeable, and worth building for. This app is meant to support that living legacy with a tool that feels current and easy to use.
