Highlights

Built to support actual tabletop play.

>_

Conduct your game how you want!

Run an in-person game of Netrunner with minimal setup, or change things up by adding or removing various trackers.
Two players on one device? One player only?
No problem!

</>

Track only what you need

Keep things simple with click tracking alone, or expand into tags, MU, and other details as the deck or matchup calls for it. Or use the app as only a click tracker, that is fine, too!

✦

Faction-driven presentation

Parallax effects, faction colors, thematic iconography, and fun textures make the game state easier to parse and more fun to look at.

🔍

Integrated card viewer

Browse the card pool in high resolution, inspect card details, and explore flavor and historical variants from inside the app.

Community built

Superconducting Hub is centered on Null Signal Games releases of Netrunner and uses community assets under the relevant guidelines.
